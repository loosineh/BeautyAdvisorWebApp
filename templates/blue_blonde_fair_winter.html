Light Winter: Soft + Cold

For your winter makeup look that complements your blue eyes, blonde hair, and fair skin, it is better to do cool-toned eyeshadow like grays and blues, enhanced with a touch of silver shimmer in the inner corners. Also, use navy or charcoal eyeliner to define your eyes and a black or dark brown mascara for added drama.

In addition, the ideal makeup palette involves choosing foundation with cool undertones that match the fair complexion. Emphasize your striking blue eyes with cool-toned eyeshadows, such as grays, silvers, deep purples, or navy blues, creating a captivating smoky eye effect. Define your eyes with dark eyeliner, and use volumizing black mascara for added drama.
