Cool Summer: Cool + Soft

The natural makeup of the Light Summer color type is light and fresh. You don't need a lot of makeup to achieve a natural look. All you need are neutral shades for the most beautiful makeup look! You can also use a pink lip gloss, and black or brown mascara. Adding some pink liquid blush would also look great on you! Use cool, silvery products, highlighters, powder, instead of black, look for charcoal grey or soft dark blue mascara and eyeliner that will fabulously match your misty beauty!

In addition, add some setting spray and cream products for a nice, dewy look for the summer time!
