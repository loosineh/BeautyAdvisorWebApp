Light Fall: Light + Cool

The natural makeup of the Light Fall color type is light and fresh. You don't need a lot of makeup to achieve a natural look. All you need are neutral shades for the most beautiful makeup look! You can also use a coral/peach lipstick, and mascara. It also works if you use neutral eyeshadow colors. You can also add some brown eyeliner and brown mascara to complete the look! You can also use a bronzer or light contour for natural makeup.

Soft Autumn Inspirations

In addition, the creamy rose blush is fabulous because you have a fair tone skin color, so you can experiment with shades of orange, coral, or peach. For example, you can use a light coral lip gloss, peach blush, and neutral brown eye makeup. Instead of black, look for shades of orange and brown, more neutral shades for the fall season.
