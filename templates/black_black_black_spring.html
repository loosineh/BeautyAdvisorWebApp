Light Spring: Light + Warm

For a captivating spring makeup look that complements black hair, black eyes, and a deep skin tone, consider a radiant foundation matching your skin tone, paired with warm blush tones like deep oranges or berries for a natural flush. Enhance your eyes with earthy eyeshadow shades such as copper or warm browns, and define them with a black or brown eyeliner. Achieve bold, luscious lashes with a volumizing mascara in black. Experiment with vibrant lip colors like deep reds or plums in matte or satin finishes for a striking effect.

In addition, Highlight your features with a gold or bronze-toned highlighter, and set your makeup in place with a long-lasting setting spray. This combination of warm, rich hues and bold accents will bring out the natural beauty of your features during the spring season.
