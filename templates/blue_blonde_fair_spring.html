Light Spring: Light + Warm

The natural makeup of the Light Spring color type is light and fresh. You don't need a lot of makeup to achieve a natural look. All you need is minimal eye makeup and light, fresh lipstick! You can also use a coral/peach lipstick or lip gloss and mascara. It also works if you use slightly bright, subtle colors. It is hard to avoid black, but use brown mascara instead.

In addition, the dewy, glossy finish is stunning on you, feel free to use highlighter. Coral or peach blush gives the face a healthy color. Your makeup will look brilliant if you use pastel ice cream colors as well. The lip glosses are graet, try a clean, golden color for the daytime look.
