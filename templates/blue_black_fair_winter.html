Cool Winter: Bright + Cool

The natural makeup of the Light Winter color type is light and fresh. You don't need a lot of makeup to achieve a natural look. All you need are powdered products for a nice, matte look for the winter. You can also use a hot pink blush. It also works if you use neutral eyeshadow colors. You can also add some white eyeliner and black mascara to complete the look!

In addition, the hot pink blush is fabulous because you have a fair-tone skin color, and pink looks wonderful in the winter time! You can also use different shades of pink lip glosses that would look perfect on you! Use black mascara, and some white eyeliner in your waterline for a snowy look for the winter.
