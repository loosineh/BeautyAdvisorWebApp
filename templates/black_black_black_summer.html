Summer: Bright + Warm

For a radiant summer look that complements black eyes, black hair, and black skin, opt for a lightweight and dewy makeup routine. Start with a hydrating primer to create a smooth canvas, followed by a luminous foundation like the Fenty Beauty Eaze Drop Blurring Skin Tint, offering buildable coverage while letting your natural skin shine through. Enhance your eyes with warm-toned eyeshadows; the Juvia's Place The Nubian Eyeshadow Palette is perfect for creating sultry and vibrant eye looks. Add a pop of color to your lips with a coral or peach lipstick such as the MAC Cosmetics Matte Lipstick in "Tropic Tonic."

In addition, finish the look with a sun-kissed glow using the Fenty Beauty Sun Stalk'r Instant Warmth Bronzer. Don't forget to set your makeup with a refreshing setting spray, like the Urban Decay All Nighter Ultra Matte Setting Spray, to keep you looking flawless in the summer heat.
