Light Summer: Light + Cool

The natural makeup of the Light Summer color type is soft and delicate. Get the most out of your ethereal beauty, your light skin with a translucent or slightly shiny base. Also, the pale silver or gold highlighter is perfect for you! Try a sophisticated style instead of strong makeup. You need minimal eye makeup and light, soft, lipstick.

In addition, the creamy rose blush is fabulous because you have a fair tone skin color, so you can experiment with shades of rose gold, light coral, or peach. For example, you can use a light pink lipstick, rosy blush, and subtle eye makeup. Instead of black, look for shades of grey, medium charcoal grey, and soft navy blue colors.
