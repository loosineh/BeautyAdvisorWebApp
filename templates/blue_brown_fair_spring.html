Light Spring: Light + Cool

The natural makeup of the Light Spring color type is light and fresh. You can also use a soft pink lip product, and add some brown mascara. Also, for your beautiful combination of blue eyes, brown hair, and fair skin during the spring season, embrace the freshness of the season with pastel eyeshadows like light pinks, lavender, or soft greens. These shades will complement your blue eyes and add a touch of warmth to your look. Opt for a rosy or peachy blush to give your fair skin a healthy and natural flush, adding a youthful glow to your overall makeup. Choose lip colors in shades of coral, pink, or nude to harmonize with your fair skin tone and balance the vibrancy of your eye makeup.

In addition, a liquid highlighter would make your look more dewy for the spring! Define your eyes with brown mascara and eyeliner for a softer look that enhances your brown hair and makes your blue eyes pop. Remember, these are just guidelines, and makeup is a form of self-expression, so feel free to experiment and find what makes you feel confident and beautiful!
