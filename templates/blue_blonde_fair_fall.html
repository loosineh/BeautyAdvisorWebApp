Soft Autumn: Soft + Warm

The natural makeup of the Soft Autumn color type is delicate, dusty, and sophisticated. You can do'nt need strong makeup to be beautiful! Brown and beige shades are great. Aim for a matte finish with just a slight sheen. You can also use a bronzer or light contour for natural makeup, but use only fine layers.

In addition, earthy pink, coffee brown, sienna or olive shades in the subtle smoky or smudge eye Makeup are wonderful. For natural makeup, you can combine a nude lipstick with subtly smoky eye makeup and a little bronzer.
