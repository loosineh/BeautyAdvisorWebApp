Bright Spring: Light + Warm

The natural makeup of the Light Spring color type is light and fresh. You don't need a lot of makeup to achieve a natural look. All you need are neutral shades for the most beautiful makeup look! You can also use a coral/peach lipstick, and mascara. It also works if you use neutral eyeyeshadow colors. You can also add some brown eyeliner and brown mascara to complete the look!
